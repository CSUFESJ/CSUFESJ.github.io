Prize money

First prize: $6,000
Second prize: $4,000
Third prize: $2,000.

Team

Form a group of 4 - 6 ECS students to develop a conceptual engineering or computer science solution to a current social justice issue.

Support

Student teams will be guided by ECS faculty to seek mentorship from CSUF alumni, industry partners, and non-profits involved in the project. Students will have an opportunity to attend workshops on entrepreneurship, crafting a business plan, and drafting a proposal in the months leading up to the competition.

Presentation

Pitch your idea to a panel of industry professionals to wint grant money for further development.
