Schedule

The competition is scheduled on March 16, 2023 at TSU Pavilion B from 1:00 - 5:00 pm. The competition is inline with the university's Social Justice Week.

Judging

The panel of judges will come from industry and academia with experience in social justice and/or technology.

Guests

We will invite people in the social justice space who may be interested in your work and could open doors to future collaboration.
