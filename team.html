Team

Form a group of 4 - 6 ECS students to identify a social justice issue and conceptualize an Engineering or Computer Science solution to address it. The team will work with an ECS mentor to develop their ideas and conceptualize their solutions. They will also attend workshops to guide them through the whole process.

Social Justice Issues

Here are some social justice issues you might consider for your projects

Climate equality
Access to public transportation
Homelessness
Access to healthy food/clean water
Income gap
